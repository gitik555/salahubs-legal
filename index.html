Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

SalaHubs ("we," "us," "the app") helps Muslims find and book welcoming places to pray. We built SalaHubs to respect you. This policy explains, in plain language, what we collect, why, and your choices.

The short version:
  • We collect the minimum needed to help you find and book a place to pray.
  • We never sell your data, and we never show third-party ad networks.
  • Worshippers use SalaHubs free, forever.

What we collect

Account information. If you create an account, we collect your email address and name. Your password is handled securely by Google Firebase Authentication and is never visible to us. You can also continue as a guest, which creates an anonymous account with no personal details.

Location. With your permission, we use your device location to show prayer hubs and mosques near you, calculate accurate prayer times, and point the Qibla compass toward the Kaaba. Your coordinates are sent to the Aladhan API (prayer times) and OpenStreetMap / Overpass (mosque locations) to return results. We do not use your location to track you, and we do not sell it. You can deny or revoke location access in iOS Settings; some features will then be limited.

Bookings & listings. When you book a space, we store the booking (the hub, prayer, time, your display name, and status) in Google Firebase so it can reach the host of that space. If you are a host, we store your listing details (name, address, and map location).

On-device data. Your favorites, prayer log, reviews, punctuality/trust score, any profile photo, and phone number are stored on your device and are not uploaded to our servers unless a feature (like bookings) requires it.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.
  • We do not run third-party advertising networks, and we never place ads on prayer screens.
  • We do not collect analytics or advertising identifiers.

Third-party services we rely on

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database) — governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Aladhan API — prayer-time calculations from your coordinates.
  • OpenStreetMap / Overpass API — mosque ("Masaajid") locations.
  • Apple — for reverse-geocoding your location into a city name for display.

Data retention & your choices

  • You can delete your account by contacting us; we will remove your account and associated bookings.
  • You can clear on-device data (prayer log, favorites) in the app, or by deleting the app from your device.
  • To request a copy of your data or ask a privacy question, email support@salahubs.app.

Children

SalaHubs is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the date above and, for significant changes, notify you in the app.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@salahubs.app.
